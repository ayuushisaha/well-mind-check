Anxiety Disorders

Excessive worry, restlessness, and panic attacks.

Behavioral and Emotional Disorders in Children

Includes ADHD, ODD, anxiety. Early intervention is key.

Bipolar Affective Disorder

Extreme mood swings between highs (mania) and lows (depression).

Depression

Persistent sadness, fatigue, and lack of interest.

Dissociative Disorders

Disconnection between thoughts, identity, and memory.

Eating Disorders

Anorexia, bulimia, binge eating—emotional and physical health risks.

Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

Intrusive thoughts and repetitive behaviors or rituals.

Paranoia

Persistent distrust or suspicion of others without reason.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

Trauma-induced flashbacks, avoidance, and hyperarousal.

Psychosis

Hallucinations, delusions, and distorted thinking.

Schizophrenia

Chronic disorder with hallucinations, delusions, and disordered thinking.
